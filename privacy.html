Privacy Policy

MTC Counter · iOS App

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

MTC Counter ("the App") is a church attendance tracking app developed by John George. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

The short version: MTC Counter collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

MTC Counter does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Any attendance data or counts you enter

Data Storage

All data you enter into the App — including attendance counts, session history, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

MTC Counter does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App makes no network requests and does not communicate with any external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is safe for use in any environment.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security is governed by your device's own security settings (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with a passcode.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
