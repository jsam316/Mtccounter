About the App

Built for Church Communities

MTC Counter is a lightweight attendance tracking app designed for Mar Thoma Church congregations. Quickly count and record attendance for Sunday services, prayer meetings, and special events — with full English and Malayalam language support.

No account needed. No data sent to the cloud. Everything stays on your device.
